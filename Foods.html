Foods

Favourite foods from the respective city

Falafel

Falafel is a Middle Eastern dish made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, seasoned with herbs and spices, and deep-fried. It's a flavorful and vegan-friendly option often served in pita bread with tahini sauce and fresh vegetables.

Fish and Chips

Fish and chips is a classic British dish featuring battered and fried fish, typically cod or haddock, served with thick-cut, golden-brown fries. This beloved comfort food is often enjoyed with a side of mushy peas and a sprinkle of malt vinegar for a satisfying and iconic meal.

Pelmeni

Pelmeni are Russian dumplings traditionally filled with a mixture of ground meat, such as beef or pork, and wrapped in thin dough. These bite-sized parcels are typically boiled or steamed and served with sour cream or butter, making them a comforting and hearty dish in Russian cuisine.

Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian delicacy made from fermented rice and urad dal batter, cooked into a thin, crispy crepe-like pancake. It's often served with coconut chutney and sambar, making it a versatile dish enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

New York-Style Pizza

New York-Style pizza is renowned for its thin, foldable slices topped with a generous layer of cheese and a variety of toppings, offering a deliciously iconic taste of the city's culinary scene. It's a quintessential New York experience enjoyed by locals and visitors alike.

Maple Syrup

Maple syrup is a sweet and natural delicacy made from the sap of sugar maple trees, primarily found in North America. Known for its rich, caramel-like flavor, it's a popular topping for pancakes, waffles, and a variety of desserts.
